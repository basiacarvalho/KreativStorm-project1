About us

Our story began in 2019 when we decided to create a website that will help people learn how to code. We are a team of 3 people who are passionate about coding and want to share our knowledge with others. We believe that everyone can learn how to code, and we are here to help you with that.

Our mission is to help people learn how to code and to make coding more accessible to everyone. We want to make coding more fun and interesting for everyone.

Our vision is to create a community of people who are passionate about coding and want to learn more about it. We want to create a place where people can learn how to code and share their knowledge with others.
