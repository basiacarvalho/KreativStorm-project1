Code Studio Front-End Developer Course

We provide a great learning experience that bridges the gap between theoretical education, as well as, practical background. Our mission is to equip those who start their journey in programming with knowledge and tools needed to excel in their future goals.

Basic information about our course

  • Is this course for me?

    Our course strive for those who would like to start their adventure with the programming but aren't sure where to start, as the coding world can seem a bit overwhelming at the beginning with all its richness and abundance of various areas.

  • Why CodeStudio course?

    Through our innovative program, students get the chance to learn sufficient skills, collaborate with our professional staff of educators and the opportunity to work on group projects based on everyday industry tasks.

  • What is the course outline?

    The course is a four months experience, divided into four core modules: HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React JS, in order to arm you with the essential skills for programming success. Each module lasts one month and divides further into two weeks of theoretical background and two weeks of practice and group projects.

Benefits that we offer :

  • Step by step learning.

  • Experienced staff of educators.

  • Theoretical and practical knowledge.

  • Engaging team projects.

  • A great educational platform.

  • A course completion certificate.
